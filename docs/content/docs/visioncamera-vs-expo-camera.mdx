### TL;DR

Expo Camera is the Expo SDK camera view. Pick it for Expo Go, Web, and fast Expo setup.

VisionCamera is a native camera framework. Pick it for camera-first apps, pro capture controls, realtime frames/ML, depth, HDR/RAW, multi-cam, custom outputs, or native plugins.

VisionCamera also has a declarative `<Camera />` API; the extra surface area comes from optional native camera features.

### Expo projects

Expo Camera is an Expo SDK package and runs in Expo Go.
VisionCamera is a third-party native package; in Expo, follow ["Getting Started"](/docs) and use `expo prebuild`, `expo run:*`, or a development build.

### Note on speed

VisionCamera is designed for high-performance native camera pipelines, but this page does not include independent Expo-Camera-vs-VisionCamera benchmarks.
